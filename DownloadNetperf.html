Download Netperf

From here you can download copies of netperf and other benchmarks.

There are at least two ways to obtain netperf. The first, ftp.netperf.org will allow you to download tar/zip files of released sources. The second would be to point a subversion SCM client at a tagged revision in the source repository. If you want the very latest netperf bits you would point your subversion client at the top-of-trunk. You may also find some places with copies or mirrors by searching at the search engine of your choice.
